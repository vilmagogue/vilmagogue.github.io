Introduction

PacificToday weather information center is the most reliable source of weather update. It's a one-stop source for tropical storm and typhoon information that tracks its movement and location. The only weather station that is relaible, current and more accurate in weather update.

Purpose and Objective

Its purpose is to deliver accurate weather forecast-locally and abroad. Staying connected to the most reliable weather forecast will keep the people informed of the weather conditions around the Globe. It is their mission to protect lives, to give accurate and reliable news to the people locally and internationally, and to avoid losses - people, properties and assets.
