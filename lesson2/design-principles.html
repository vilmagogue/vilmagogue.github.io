Hick's Law

Applying Hick’s Law is giving the users the choice to decide an ideas in a simplier way. The more choices the users see or being presented , the longer it will take them to reach a decision.

Hick's Law is a design that is accessible to the users, easy to understand and make advance interpretation and decision.

The concept of Proximity are grouped together and closely related, It makes the design more distinguish and organize. The users will have a quick access as he search and avoid any confusion to items category.
